Nintendo Entertainment System

Classic 8-bit action

The NES, released in 1983, revolutionized gaming and became an iconic console. With classics like Super Mario Bros. and The Legend of Zelda, it captured the hearts of players worldwide. Its impact on the industry is immeasurable, revitalizing gaming after the crash of 1983. The NES holds a special place in gaming history, symbolizing the start of a new era of interactive entertainment.

Super NES

Crazy power!

The Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES) is a legendary console that revolutionized gaming. Released in 1990, it offered improved graphics, immersive sound, and a stellar game library.

With iconic titles like Super Mario World and The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past, the SNES left an indelible mark on gaming history. The SNES is an enduring symbol of Nintendo's commitment to excellence and its impact on the gaming industry.

Nintendo 64

3D graphics? No way!

The Nintendo 64 (N64), launched in 1996, revolutionized gaming with its 3D graphics and innovative gameplay.

Its multiplayer capabilities and unique controller design made it a favorite for group gaming. The N64 remains a cherished console, celebrated for its innovation and unforgettable games.

Nintendo GameCube

Our favorite Square console!

The Nintendo GameCube, launched in 2001, made a lasting impact with its unique design and impressive games. The GameCube's multiplayer capabilities and innovative features provided countless hours of fun.

Despite not matching commercial success, it remains beloved for its distinctive charm and memorable gaming experiences.

Nintendo Wii

Everyone has one!!!

The Nintendo Wii, released in 2006, transformed the gaming landscape with its innovative motion controls. The Wii's extensive game library included popular titles like Mario Kart Wii, and Twilight Princess.

With its social and family-friendly focus, the Wii brought people together for memorable gaming sessions and remains a cherished console in gaming history.

Game Boy Color

Color gaming on the go!

The Game Boy Color, launched in 1998, was a handheld gaming sensation. Its vibrant color display breathed new life into portable gaming.

With a vast library of games, including Pokémon Crystal, The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening DX, and Super Mario Bros. Deluxe, the Game Boy Color offered endless hours of entertainment on the go. Its compact design and long battery life made it a beloved companion for gaming enthusiasts everywhere.

Game Boy Advance

Biggest library of games ever!

The Game Boy Advance, launched in 2001, took portable gaming to new heights. With enhanced graphics and a vast library of games including Pokémon FireRed and The Minish Cap, it offered unforgettable adventures on the go.

Its compatibility with previous Game Boy titles added to its appeal. The Game Boy Advance remains an iconic handheld console, beloved for its gaming excellence.
